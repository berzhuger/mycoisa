ABOUT THE COMPANY

With the main office and warehouses in Novo Hamburgo, state of Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil, Liderkoll provides technical and commercial support for the sale of all kinds of leather in Brazil and South America. The company is also represented in the world's main leather business centers by its associated offices.

Liderkoll represents companies that are renowned in the leather business and offers its clients technical and commercial assistance on orders made, from the development of samples, production control, documentation, to logistics and technical inspection with quality control performed prior to each shipment.

We strive to offer our clients and suppliers with technical and commercial counseling thus consolidating trustful and successful partnerships.

PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

We are able to supply all types of leather, for shoes, bags, garment, upholstery, and authomotive from various origins, always searching for the best and suitable option that applies to the necessity of our costumers.

Green and Salted hides
Wet Blue hides
Crust / Semi Finished hides
Finished hides
Hair on Hides
Technical articles such as waterproof, fireproof etc.
